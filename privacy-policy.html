English Version

XCalculator ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, retain, and protect your data within our mobile app with package name com.fyapps.xcalculator.

1. Data We Collect

When you use our application, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Account Info: If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your email address and unique user ID to securely manage your account and synchronize app preferences.
  • App Preferences: Your selected settings, currency options, decimal precision, and favorite preferences.
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising identifiers (AdID / IDFA) collected by Google AdMob solely for serving non-intrusive generic ads and resolving performance analytics.
Crucial Note on Calculation History:

We DO NOT collect or sync your calculation history to the cloud. All calculation inputs, histories, lists, and custom formula creations are stored strictly locally on your own device. This data is never sent to, processed by, or saved on our cloud servers.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • To maintain your cloud-saved preferences and display settings across your signed-in devices.
  • To provide, secure, and maintain core calculator application settings.
  • To serve non-intrusive advertisements utilizing Google AdMob.
  • To prevent fraud and maintain network and app terminal security.

3. Core Data Retention & Deletion Policy

How Long and Where We Keep Your Data:
  • Active Registered Accounts: Your basic profile credentials and app preferences are securely stored on Google Firebase servers as long as your user account remains active.
  • Data Erasure Policy: When you delete your account (via the app settings or our deletion console) or request manual deletion, your profile information and cloud settings are permanently and irreversibly removed from our active databases and backup systems within exactly 30 days.
  • Calculation History Storage: Since calculation logs are stored 100% locally on your device, you have instant control. You can clear them anytime inside the app's History screen. Deleting the app from your device also instantly and permanently wipes all local calculation records.

4. Data Sharing & Transmission

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share processed preferences or analytics information with trusted third-party providers (Google Firebase and Google AdMob/AdSense) strictly for providing backend services, analytical reporting, and serving advertisements as authorized by this policy.

5. Data Security

We implement rigid security standards. All network transmissions of user credentials/preferences are fully encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS and Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) / TLS 1.3 protocols to prevent unauthorized interception.

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion Link

You have full ownership of your data. You can terminate your profile instantly inside the app Settings. Alternatively, you can request full erasure of your account details on our Account Deletion Page or email your request to ferhatyalin@gmail.com.

7. Contact

For any questions regarding your data privacy or retention practices, contact us at: ferhatyalin@gmail.com
